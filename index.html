About Me

Dr. Ismail Kaoud is a Professor of Numerical Analysis at the Islamic University in Madinah, Saudi Arabia. He holds a PhD in Pure Mathematics from Ain Shams University and Duisburg University, Germany. He has extensive experience in academic research, specializing in computational mathematics, boundary value problems, and fractional differential equations.

Academic Background

- M.Sc. in Pure Mathematics (Numerical Analysis) - Ain Shams University, Egypt (1990)
- PhD in Pure Mathematics (Numerical Analysis) - Ain Shams University, Egypt & Duisburg University, Germany (1997)
